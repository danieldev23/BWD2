Plan a tree

Tree Planting Project

A tree will be planted for every $0.5 you support us. Let's join hands to save the green lungs of the earth

  • Our goal is to plant 100.000 trees in 1 year
  • More than 100 people have participated in this project
  • More than 50.000 trees have been planted
  • Trees have been planted in 5 provinces and cities: Thanh Hoa, Nghe An, Quang Binh, Hue, Da Nang

clean river

River Cleaning Project

Garbage in rivers will pollute water sources and worsen urban landscapes.We hope to bring a beautiful urban landscape in the eyes of residents and international friends.

  • Our goal is to clean 10,000 meters of river in 1 year
  • More than 250 people have participated in this project
  • More than 4,000 meters of river have been cleaned
  • Currently we have cleaned up some rivers and canals in Da Nang and Quang Nam

Clear River

Beach Cleanup Project

Trash on beaches pollutes the environment, harms marine life, and affects tourism. We aim to create pristine beaches for everyone to enjoy.

  • Our goal is to clean 5,000 meters of beach in 1 year
  • Over 300 volunteers have joined our cause
  • We have already cleaned 2,000 meters of beach
  • Efforts are ongoing in Da Nang and Quang Nam

We founded NaNangGreen

In the first days, our team only had 5 people, working with passion and determination to build DaNangGreen. Without fancy offices, we meet in small cafes, share ideas and make plans. Despite many difficulties, we always believe in the ultimate goal. We have overcome many challenges, from planning the details to finding funding. Step by step, we have achieved the first results, turning DaNangGreen from a project

The first river we cleaned

We have committed to cleaning the river with passion and love for the environment. The river was once an important source of life for the whole community, but over time, it has been greatly influenced by economic development and human activities. Waste, pollutants and the depletion of water resources are urgent problems that we are determined to solve.
