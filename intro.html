DANANG GREEN TEAM

Our volunteer team is a group of passionate young people working together towards the goal of improving and enhancing the quality of life in the community.


DaNang Green Team was founded with the spirit of "Living to Give". Our volunteer team always aims for practical and meaningful activities such as planting trees to protect the environment, organizing free classes for underprivileged children, visiting and assisting elderly people living alone, as well as participating in charity campaigns and emergency support efforts.

Core values

Responsibility

We take responsibility for the environment through our efforts in collecting and recycling waste.

Collaboration

We believe that only together can we make a difference. We are always open to collaboration.

Dedication

We are tirelessly committed to creating a clean environment and protecting the health and well-being of our community.

Innovation

We are eager to embrace and apply new methods to enhance environmental protection efforts.

VISION & MISSION

Vision

We aim to transform Da Nang and Vietnam into model regions for environmental protection by improving environmental quality. Our goal is to inspire a wave of environmental consciousness that spreads nationwide and globally, fostering a generation that values and actively protects our planet.

Mission

DaNang Green Team is dedicated to not only collecting and recycling river waste but also raising community awareness about environmental protection. By promoting sustainability and community action, we seek to expand our impact throughout Vietnam and the world, encouraging proactive steps towards a cleaner, healthier environment.

Short-Term Goals

  • - Enhance operational efficiency through strategic measures.
  • - Continue maintaining the DaNang Green activities.
  • - Attract and convey the environmental protection message.
  • - Transition from a spontaneous movement to a professional organization.

    • Long-Term Goals

  • - Address environmental issues like waste, and education awareness about environmental protection.
  • - Establish a professional non-profit organization.
  • - Apply science and technology, digital transformation into projects.
  • - Rescue and revive all rivers and canals within the next 3 years.
